🍽️ Where to Eat?

Filter and get a random pick

📖 How to Use

▼
1
Pick a Place

Use the filters at the top to narrow down by Type, Cuisine, Price Range, or Location. Then hit 🎲 Pick a Place to get a random suggestion from your filtered list.

2
Group Mode

Deciding with friends? Hit 👥 Group Mode to get multiple options at once. Use the dropdown to choose how many (3, 4, or 5). Hit New Options to reshuffle.

3
Browse the List

Tap All Matches to see every restaurant that fits your current filters, grouped by cuisine. A green dot means there's a note — tap the restaurant to reveal it.

4
Visit Counter

Each list item has a 🍽️ counter with + / − buttons, and results have a ✓ Been Here button. The more times you've visited a place, the less often it gets suggested — but it's never fully excluded, so you can still go back to favourites.

5
Add or Edit a Place

Tap ➕ Add a Place to add a new restaurant, or the ✏️ button on any list item to edit or delete it. Changes save straight to the database — refresh to see them.

6
Two Tabs

Switch between 🍽️ Where to Eat (places you go to) and ⭐ Want to Try (your bucket list) using the tabs up top. Each has its own list and picker. When you finally visit a wishlist place, pick it and hit 🍽️ Mark as Tried to move it over to your main list automatically.

7
Dark Mode

Tap the 🌙 / ☀️ button in the top-right corner to switch between light and dark themes. Your choice is remembered.
