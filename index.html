🍽️ Where to Eat?

Filter and get a random pick

📖 How to Use

▼
1
Pick a Place

Use the filters at the top to narrow down by Type, Cuisine, Price Range, or Location. Then hit 🎲 Pick a Place to get a random suggestion from your filtered list.

2
Group Mode

Deciding with friends? Hit 👥 Group Mode to get multiple options at once. Use the dropdown to choose how many (3, 4, or 5). Hit New Options to reshuffle.

3
Browse the List

Tap All Matches to see every restaurant that fits your current filters, sorted A–Z. A green dot means there's a note — tap the restaurant to reveal it.

4
Add a Place

Tap ➕ Add a Place to submit a new restaurant. Fill in the details and hit Add to List — it goes straight into the database. Refresh the page to see it appear.

5
Updating the List

Your restaurant list lives in Google Sheets. You can add, edit, or delete rows there anytime. Just refresh the app and the changes will load automatically.
